About Us

We as an organisation wholeheartedly guide our students who are attached to the endless loophole of stereotypical course choices. We aim to make students aware of prevalent course selections that don't pique their interest. Unsway is here to support students worldwide in inspiring them to follow their passions and offering eclectic yet cognitive course selection. Unsway's main objective is to mentor/teach students in various courses, making their future route as simple as ever.

What is Exosway?

Exosway is an online competition revolving around the theme exo-planets. The main idea of this competition is to emphasize on the concepts of exo-planets existing outside the conventional solar system. Thus highlighting and encouraging individuals such as hih school students, enthusiasts to create innovative ideas , drive through implementation and to learn knowledge outside the box. It includes contribution from 5 clubs in unsway namely Finance and Entrepreneurship club for commerce, Coding and AI club for tech enthusiasts and PSVC club for aspiring literature enthusiasts. Each competition from each club will involve wonderful opportunities for students to instill creative energy and criticial thinking and encrouages students to win exciting prizes. This event will involve guest speakers from diverse fields to provide students holistic perspective.
